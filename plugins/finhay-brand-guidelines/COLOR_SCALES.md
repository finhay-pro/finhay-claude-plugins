## Color Pairing Guidelines

### Background & Text Combinations

**Dark Backgrounds**:
- Background: Neutral 900 (#0D0E10) or 800 (#171719)
- Primary Text: Neutral 100 (#F8F8F8)
- Secondary Text: Neutral 500 (#666666) or #999999

**Light Backgrounds**:
- Background: Neutral 100 (#F8F8F8) or White (#FFFFFF)
- Primary Text: Neutral 900 (#0D0E10)
- Secondary Text: Neutral 500 (#666666)

### Accent Combinations

**Brand Accent on Dark**:
- Background: Neutral 900
- Accent: Brand 500 (#49D82F)
- Text: Neutral 100

**Brand Accent on Light**:
- Background: Neutral 100
- Accent: Brand 600 (#3AAD26)
- Text: Neutral 900

### State Color Usage

**Success**:
- Primary: Green 500 or 600
- Background: Green 100 or 200
- Border: Green 300

**Error**:
- Primary: Red 500 or 600
- Background: Red 100 or 200
- Border: Red 300

**Warning**:
- Primary: Orange 500 or Yellow 500
- Background: Orange 100 or Yellow 100
- Border: Orange 300 or Yellow 300

**Info**:
- Primary: Blue 500 or 600
- Background: Blue 100 or 200
- Border: Blue 300

## Accessibility Notes

### Contrast Requirements (WCAG AA)

- Normal text (< 18pt): Minimum 4.5:1 contrast
- Large text (≥ 18pt or ≥ 14pt bold): Minimum 3:1 contrast
- UI components: Minimum 3:1 contrast

### Recommended Pairings

**Pass WCAG AA for Normal Text**:
- Neutral 900 on Neutral 100: 18.45:1 ✓
- Neutral 100 on Neutral 900: 18.45:1 ✓
- Brand 600 on Neutral 100: 4.52:1 ✓
- Brand 500 on Neutral 900: 5.21:1 ✓
- Blue 600 on Neutral 100: 4.89:1 ✓

**Testing**: Always verify contrast ratios when creating new color combinations.
